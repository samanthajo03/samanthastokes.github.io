Samantha Stokes is a Missouri School of Journalism graduate who studied print and digital news, magazine writing and audience engagement. Her writing has appeared in Vox Magazine, the Columbia Missourian, Missouri Business Alert and Elite Daily. She also managed social media for Bustle's millennial parenting site, Romper. Samantha enjoys weird things on the internet, running, dogs and her slow cooker. This fall, she'll attend Columbia Journalism School for a master's degree in (you guessed it) journalism. In the meantime, her parents have graciously opened their Lincoln, NE home to her for the summer, which is, of course, any post-graduate's dream.
